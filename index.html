Our Mission

The mission of the Fire Department is to protect the lives and property of the people of Wake County from fires, natural disasters, and hazardous materials incidents; to save lives by providing emergency medical services; to prevent fires through prevention and education programs; and to provide a work environment that values health, wellness and cultural diversity and is free of harassment and discrimination.

Commissioner's Message

As the 12th Fire Commissioner of the Wake County Fire Department, it is my pleasure to welcome you to our website. Every day the members of our Department bravely protect life and property in this beautiful county. Our Department not only responds to emergencies, we help prevent them by getting out into the community and promoting our many educational programs.
